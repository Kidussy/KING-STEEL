Access Control Systems

SMART LOCKING
SOLUTIONS.

Beyond the door itself, King Steel offers a complete range of intelligent locking systems — from fingerprint biometrics to remotely managed commercial locks. Choose the access technology that fits your lifestyle, security level, and building type.

01
🖐
You are the key.
Biometric Locks

Grant access based on unique physiological traits. Virtually impossible to duplicate or share — your body is the credential.

  • Fingerprint scanners — fast, familiar, reliable
  • Palm vein readers — highly secure, contactless
  • 3D facial recognition — advanced anti-spoofing technology
  • Multi-user support with individual access logs
  • Ideal for high-security residential and enterprise environments
02
🔢
Secure with a PIN.
Keypad (Code) Locks

Require users to input a pre-programmed numeric PIN or password to unlock. No keys to lose, copy, or forget.

  • Numeric PIN / password access
  • Backlit keypad for easy use in any lighting
  • Temporary codes for guests or service workers
  • Tamper alarm & auto-lock options
  • Excellent for apartments, offices, and rental properties
03
📶
Smart access. Anywhere.
Smart / Wi-Fi Locks

Connect to your home network or smartphone via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. Full control from anywhere in the world, anytime.

  • Remote lock/unlock via mobile app
  • Real-time notifications & activity logs
  • Smart home integration (Google Home, Apple HomeKit)
  • Grant, revoke & manage access remotely
  • Auto-lock schedules and geofencing support
04
🪪
Tap. Access. Go.
RFID / Key Fob Locks

Utilize Radio Frequency Identification to transmit a signal. Users tap a plastic card or key fob against the sensor — no insertion, no friction.

  • RFID cards, key fobs & tags supported
  • Quick & convenient contactless access
  • Easy to issue, revoke & reprogram
  • Industry standard for hotels and corporate buildings
  • Perfect for offices, schools & apartment complexes
05
⚡
Built for strength. Designed for control.
Electromechanical & Commercial Locks

Designed for high-traffic or high-security environments. Heavy-duty mechanisms managed by centralized access control software.

  • Electric strikes — fail-safe or fail-secure configurations
  • Magnetic locks — powerful electromagnetic holding force
  • Robust cylindrical locks for industrial use
  • Seamless integration with property management software
  • Durable, reliable, built to meet safety & compliance standards
